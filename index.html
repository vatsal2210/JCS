Jain Community Services of Canada

Providing voluntary services to Canadian Jains and newcomers since 1985

"Sewa is our religion" - સેવા પરમો ધર્મ છે

At arm's length with the Jain Society of Toronto. All donations received go to JSOT.

The seeds for JCS were planted in 1976 and founded in 1985 with help from late Bapuji Mohanlal Mehta

Services and Supports

Accommodations and settling new immigrants in Canada
Job support
Visits and food delivery for people admitted to hospitals
Financial support for students & families
Mental health and specialist support for individuals & families
Shantipath and cremation services as per Jain Vidhi
Feeding hungry or homeless
Buy/sell items in good condition
Matrimonial services: JCS Matrimony / Sagapan Setu
Many more services that families may need
